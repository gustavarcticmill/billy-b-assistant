Google News (Headlines) help_outline
Generic top headlines feed with locale hardcoded in the URL:
https://news.google.com/rss?hl=en-GB&gl=GB&ceid=GB:en

To change locale, edit hl=, gl=, and ceid= directly in the URL.

Tip for topic/category pages: take a normal Google News URL and insert rss/ right after news.google.com/.
Example:
https://news.google.com/topics/CAAqJggKIiBDQkFTRWdvSUwyMHZNRGx6TVdZU0FtNXNHZ0pPVENnQVAB?hl=en-GB&gl=GB&ceid=GB%3Aen
becomes
https://news.google.com/rss/topics/CAAqJggKIiBDQkFTRWdvSUwyMHZNRGx6TVdZU0FtNXNHZ0pPVENnQVAB?hl=en-GB&gl=GB&ceid=GB%3Aen
Google News (Localized search) help_outline
Uses dynamic query with locale hardcoded in the URL:
https://news.google.com/rss/search?q={{query}}&hl=en-GB&gl=GB&ceid=GB:en

To change locale, edit hl=, gl=, and ceid= directly in the URL.

Tip for topic/category pages: take a normal Google News URL and insert rss/ right after news.google.com/.
Example:
https://news.google.com/topics/CAAqJggKIiBDQkFTRWdvSUwyMHZNRGx6TVdZU0FtNXNHZ0pPVENnQVAB?hl=en-GB&gl=GB&ceid=GB%3Aen

Open-Meteo Forecast (Amsterdam) help_outline
Free weather API endpoint. This example uses a fixed location (Amsterdam):
https://api.open-meteo.com/v1/forecast?latitude=52.3676&longitude=4.9041&timezone=Europe%2FAmsterdam

You can swap coordinates/timezone for your own city.

Docs / index:
https://open-meteo.com/en/docs
https://open-meteo.com/en/docs/geocoding-api
ESPN Scoreboard (Premier League) help_outline
ESPN scoreboard pattern: https://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/{sport}/{league}/scoreboard
Soccer (major leagues):
  • Premier League: /sports/soccer/eng.1/scoreboard
  • LaLiga: /sports/soccer/esp.1/scoreboard
  • Serie A: /sports/soccer/ita.1/scoreboard
  • Ligue 1: /sports/soccer/fra.1/scoreboard
  • Bundesliga: /sports/soccer/ger.1/scoreboard
  • Eredivisie: /sports/soccer/ned.1/scoreboard
Other major sports:
  • NFL: /sports/football/nfl/scoreboard
  • NBA: /sports/basketball/nba/scoreboard
  • MLB: /sports/baseball/mlb/scoreboard
  • NHL: /sports/hockey/nhl/scoreboard
ESPN Soccer News (Serie A) help_outline
ESPN soccer news format:
  • League format: http://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/soccer/:league/news
  • League-specific (ITA): https://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/soccer/ita.1/news
  • Global soccer news: https://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/soccer/all/news
ESPN Team Info (Napoli) help_outline
Team info example (Napoli, Serie A):
  • Team details: https://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/soccer/ita.1/teams/114
  • Find team IDs by league: http://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/soccer/:league/teams
  • Future matches (all competitions): http://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/soccer/all/teams/:team_id/schedule?fixture=true
  • Played matches (all competitions): http://site.api.espn.com/apis/site/v2/sports/soccer/all/teams/:team_id/schedule
